Our Story

Where Cuba Meets
Chicago

Cubaneo was born from a love of Cuban tradition and the vibrant energy of Chicago's Lakeview neighborhood — a place where old-world recipes meet modern creativity.

Every dish tells the story of the island — slow-roasted meats marinated in mojo, golden tostones fried to perfection, and paellas baked in wood-fired ovens. Pair any meal with one of our handcrafted cocktails or freshly blended tropical smoothies and strong Cuban coffee.

Whether you're stopping by for a casual weekday lunch, a weekend brunch with friends, or a lively dinner, Cubaneo offers an experience that's as warm as the Caribbean sun.
